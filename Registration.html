Register to receive information about our Open Days

This program introduces new students to university life, covering rules, campus facilities, and academic culture. It also includes fun activities to build friendships with peers and seniors.

Please register below to receive more information about our 2024 Undergraduate Open Days.

Our Open Days this year will take place on Wednesday 26 June, Thursday 27 June and Friday 20 September. We can't wait to welcome you and for you to look round our beautiful university city, chat to staff and students and find our first-hand what it's like to study here.

Registration for our Open Days is not compulsory, but by entering your details below you will receive lots of useful information about these events and about applying for undergraduate study at Oxford via our Choosing Oxford newsletter for prospective applicants.
