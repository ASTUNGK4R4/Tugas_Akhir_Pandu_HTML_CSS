Orientation Week (Ospek/PKKMB)
Wednesday 23 October

This program introduces new students to university life, covering rules, campus facilities, and academic culture. It also includes fun activities to build friendships with peers and seniors.

Job Fair and Career Workshop
Wednesday 23 October

These events connect students with companies and provide career development opportunities. Job fairs allow companies to recruit interns and employees, while career workshops help students refine skills such as CV writing and interview techniques.

Sports and Arts Week (POR or Cultural Arts Festival)
Wednesday 23 October

This event fosters teamwork and sportsmanship through competitions in various sports and arts. Students get the chance to showcase their talents through performances like music, dance, or theater. It also helps build stronger connections between faculties.

DISABLED ACCESS

Where possible, public events are held in venues that are easily accessible, close to accessible bathrooms, and equipped with hearing loop systems.

Please contact the Event Organiser and/or the Disability Office for accessibility information, or to give feedback on venues.
